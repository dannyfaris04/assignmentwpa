Currency Conversion

Currency conversion is the process of exchanging one currency for another at a given rate.

There are different reasons why people need to convert currencies, such as traveling, trading, investing, or sending money abroad.

The most commonly used currencies in the world are the US dollar (USD), the euro (EUR), the British pound (GBP), and the Malaysian ringgit (MYR).
